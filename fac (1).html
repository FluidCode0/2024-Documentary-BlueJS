Introduction to Bluetooth Usage

Bluetooth is a wireless technology that allows devices to communicate with each other over short distances. In BlueJS, you can leverage Bluetooth to create various functionalities and connections. Here's a basic guide on how to use Bluetooth:

  1. Turn on Bluetooth on your device.
  2. Search for nearby Bluetooth devices.
  3. Select a Bluetooth device to connect to.
  4. Establish a connection with the chosen device.
  5. Access services and characteristics for data transfer.
  6. Send and receive data using Bluetooth.
